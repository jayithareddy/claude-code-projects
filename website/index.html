About

Analytical mind,
creative spirit.

I'm someone who finds meaning at the intersection of data, design, and human behaviour.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Business Analytics at FLAME University with a minor in Digital Marketing. I spend a lot of my time thinking about how data tells stories, how AI can reshape workflows, and how products become experiences people genuinely love using.

My work spans business intelligence, AI-assisted data processing, research, and content. What threads it together is an obsession with clarity: making complex things simple, finding the pattern hiding in the noise.

Outside of the analytical world, I'm equally at home with a sketchbook, a camera, or a piano. I find that creativity and analysis aren't opposites. They're the same muscle, flexed differently. Photography in particular is something I'm building into a serious practice, and I'll have a gallery to show for it soon.
