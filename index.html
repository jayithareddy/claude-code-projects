About

Hi, I'm Jayitha. I'm genuinely excited about data, business, and how AI is changing pretty much everything right now.

I'm currently studying Business Administration with a major in Business Analytics at FLAME University, with a minor in Digital Marketing. Honestly, I picked this path because I wanted to understand how decisions actually get made in business, and data felt like the most honest answer to that. The more I learn, the more I realise how much there still is to figure out, and that kind of excites me.

I've been getting my hands dirty with business intelligence, AI tools, research, and marketing, mostly through internships where I've had to figure things out as I go. I'm really into how AI is shifting what "work" even means, and I spend probably too much time thinking about that.

Outside of all that, I paint, play piano, take photos, and travel whenever I can. I think being creative and being analytical are way more connected than people assume, at least for me they are.
